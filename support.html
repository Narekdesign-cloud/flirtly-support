Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your Apple ID at the top → Subscriptions → Flirtly → Cancel.

My analysis isn't working

Make sure you have an internet connection. If the issue persists, email us.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com

Is my data private?

Yes. All conversations are stored only on your device. We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. Conversation text is sent to OpenAI for analysis only. See our Privacy Policy.

What languages does Flirtly support?

English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, and Russian.
