Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Flirtly ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

Flirtly does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All your conversations, chat profiles, and analysis history are stored locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses OpenAI's API to analyze conversations. When you tap "Analyze", the conversation text you paste is sent to OpenAI's servers for processing. OpenAI's privacy policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

We do not send any identifying information (name, email, device ID) alongside the conversation text.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following is stored locally and never leaves your device:

  • Chat profile names
  • Conversation text you paste
  • AI-generated analysis results
  • Your settings (language, communication style)

Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all local data.

4. Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

5. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use analytics, trackers, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking. We do not track your behavior across apps or websites.

6. Children

Flirtly is not directed at children under 12.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
